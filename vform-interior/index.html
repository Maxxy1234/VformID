About V Form

V Form Interior was born from a simple belief: your home should feel like you — curated and comfortable. Based in Kuala Lumpur, our team blends modern contemporary design with warm, tactile details that make every room feel intentional.

We believe that luxury isn't about excess—it's about intention. Every material, every colour, every curve is chosen with purpose. We work closely with our clients to understand their lifestyle, their dreams, and their everyday needs, translating that into spaces that are both beautiful and livable.
